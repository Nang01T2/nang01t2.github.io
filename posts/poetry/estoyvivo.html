I exist with all my senses,
that shows me that I am still alive,
I hear strong divine songs,
the prayers of the wretched.

I hear those who want to hear themselves,
it fills my soul that impeccable company,
joined by the smell of clouds, sky and life.

The dead envy me, because I am alive
and I can see the heart of my beloved,
the soul of my friends, the spirit of the children,
The wisdom of the elders,
and the happiness of human kind.

That elixir that we can turn into:
death, sorrow and pain.
That elixir that burns the heart,
a deadly poison contrary to love.

Feel the warmth of the hug,
the deep cold of disdain,
the non-existent flavor of water
and the delight of admiring a landscape.

Remember to feel, because you still can,
you can still enjoy your favorite food,
you can still hear birds singing in the morning,
gaze at the immaculate face of the one you love,
and smell the natural incense of the purest air.

Do it! Because we still can,
because we are not dead, not yet.
