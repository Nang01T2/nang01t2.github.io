Personal blog of Kevin Kelly cofounder of Wired in 1993 and now Senior Maverick writer and photographer. Cofounder of the *All Species Foundation* later becoming part of the [Encyclopedia of Life](https://eol.org/). Similar sites are the [COL](https://www.catalogueoflife.org/), [Open Tree of Life](https://tree.opentreeoflife.org/) and the [Wikimedia Species](https://species.wikimedia.org/wiki/Main_Page). He worked on the Long Now Foundation and The Rosetta Disk that later inspired the [Rosetta Project](https://rosettaproject.org/).
