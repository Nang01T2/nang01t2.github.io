## American pop singer (Gospel and Rock - 2000)

* Jason Mraz was born in 1977 in Virginia, his Czech grandparents moved to the US in 1915, Mraz means frozen.
* After dropping from college he moved to San Diego in 1999, playing the guitar at coffee shops.
	* One day he met Toca Rivera and Ian Sheridan and they starting playing together.
	* With a heavy influence from Brazilian music in 2001 they released their first acoustic album *Live at Java Joes*
* After signing a contract with *Elektra Records* he released his debut album *Waiting for My Rocket to Come*.
	* The album contained the hit *The Remedy (I Won't Worry)*, launching his career.
	* His 2008 album *We Sing. We Dance. We Steal Things* contained his greatest hit to date *I'm Yours*.
* Aside from music he is also known as a philanthropist, surfer, photographer and yogi.
