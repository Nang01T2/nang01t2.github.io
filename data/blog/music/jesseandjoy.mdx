## Mexican pop duo (Latin and Folk - 2005) ##

* Jesse Eduardo Huerta (Vocalist and keyboard) born in 1982 and Tirzah Joy Huerta (Vocalist and guitar) born in 1986.
* The Huerta brothers were both born in Mexico City and started their music career by playing at their local church.
* In 2005 they created *Jesse and Joy* after signing with *Warner Music Latina* and releasing their debut song *Llegaste Tu*.
	* They got funding from *Sin Bandera* to release their debut album *Esta es mi vida* in 2006.
	* Their third album *¿Con quién se queda el perro?* launched in 2011 and received six Latin Grammy Awards.
	* They had succesfuly launched two World Tours and in 2019 singed with J Balvin in *Mañana Es Too Late*
	* Their album *Aire* released in 2020 includes hits like *Tanto*, *Lo Nuestro Vale Mas* and *Alguien Mas*
* They are philanthropists that have donated to *UnidosUS*, a group that inspires Latin communities in the US to vote.
