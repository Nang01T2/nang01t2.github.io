Para Larissa

Probablemente 2012

Light blue, blue sky,
blue peace, blue from your eyes.

Do not let me drown in them,
because I see a soul that suffers,
a smile that silently guards what you think,
and a golden flower that never bloomed.

Planted in Eden,
eager to breathe its own perfume.

Prisoner of your own petals,
if I drown in your eyes,
it was not in vain,
life was worth it,
if I could briefly contemplate,
how beautiful you were.
