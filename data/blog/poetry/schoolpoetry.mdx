**Poem based on an image**

![Le Baiser Kiss](/static/images/poetry/Le_Baiser.jpg)

> The kiss, sculpture Le Baiser by Auguste Rodin in 1882. Originally created as part of the gates of hell sculpture, it represents Francesca da Rimini an Italian woman that got in love with the younger brother of his husband Paolo, her husband found out and killed them both. In the sculpture their lips don't touch, they symbolize the prohibited, the secret desires and fully giving yourself to love.

<div className="italic">
Infierno, caigo a ti de espaldas,<br/>
de tu rosa viví por mucho tiempo.<br/>
Derrame mis manos por el mango de tu espalda,<br/>
y dije lo prohibido a oscuras, sin cielo.<br/>

Que la eternidad nos espere por un momento,<br/>
no vaya ser que nos encuentre a solas<br/>
o que el aroma que habías derretido en mi piel,<br/>
se canse de invadir mis horas.<br/>
</div>

<div className = "italic">
Hell, I fall with my back to you, <br/>
from your rose I've lived long enough. <br/>
I've shifted my hands down the handle of your back, <br/>
said what is forbidden in the dark, without heaven. <br/>

May eternity wait for us, just for a moment, <br/>
else he may be the one that finds us side by side <br/>
or the aroma that you had melted on my skin, <br/>
gets tired of invading my every hour. <br/>
</div>

**Poem based on a movie (Kill Bill)**

<div className="italic">
Estoy en blanco. Soy el blanco de la nieve que me acobija.<br/>
Congelo con el frio los instantes y me vuelvo su contorno.<br/>
Veo mi enemiga a los ojos, es como si me viera a mi misma.<br/>
Dejamos que el silencio nos consuma sin despertar en vano.<br/>
Volaron libres los sollozos huérfanos de nuestras katanas.<br/>
Orígenes y partidas nuevas. Poder bailar y morir de noche.<br/>
</div>

<div className = "italic">
I'm blank. I am the white of the snow that shelters me. <br/>
I freeze in the moments of cold and become its contour. <br/>
I see my enemy in the eyes and it's like seeing myself. <br/>
We let silence consume us, we aren't waking up in vain. <br/>
The orphan sobs of our katanas flew freed from torment. <br/>
Origins and new roads signal to dance and die at night. <br/>
</div>

**Poem based on a fear**

<div className="italic">
La última llamada.<br/>
Atención.<br/>
Final del aire, tiempo y razón.<br/>
Soy.    Punto.<br/>
(Así me escondo del peligro)<br/>

Tengo miedo a caer.<br/>
¿Se llama muerte?<br/>
Como un cometa,<br/>
desmayarme en la acera.<br/>
Tengo miedo a caer.<br/>

</div>

<div className = "italic">
The last call.<br/>
Attention.<br/>
This is the end of the air, time and reason.<br/>
I am. Period. <br/>
(This is how I hide from danger) <br/>

I'm afraid of falling. <br/>
Is it called death? <br/>
Like a comet, <br/>
pass out on the sidewalk.<br/>
I'm afraid of falling.<br/>

</div>

**Poem based on levels of language**

<div className="italic">
Entierra aquí mi cofre divino,<br/>
sin aire, nuestra droga es eterna.<br/>

Tus golpes son el rojo de mi alma,<br/>
darte a luz desde un violín sublime.<br/>

Solo quiero más agua para recordar,<br/>
el chocolate que dejo tu nombre.<br/>
</div>

<div className = "italic">
Bury here my divine chest, <br/>
without air, our drug is eternal. <br/>

Your strokes are the red of my soul, <br/>
in a sublime violin I gave birth to you.<br/>

I just want more water to remember, <br/>
that chocolate that your name left. <br/>
</div>

**Poem based on an emotion (Awe)**

<div className="italic">
Burbujear de la nada y pasar junto a ella.<br/>
El telón de mi obra es la briza del día.<br/>
Ahí es el pueblo natal de mis expectativas.<br/>
Cerca de la cotidianeidad estropeada.<br/>
Otro tragaluz que deja a mi sombra arruinada.<br/>
</div>

***

<div className = "italic">
Bubble out of nowhere and walk just past her.<br/>
The curtain of my work is the breeze of the day. <br/>
That's the hometown of my expectations. <br/>
Just past the spoiled everyday life. <br/>
Another skylight leaves my shadow ruined. <br/>
</div>
