Probablemente 2012

You, who take my breath away,
as stubborn as the hurricane,
just the seeing of you destroys me.

Your ridges when walking feel
like mountains,
Without water, without sun,
just a small breeze.

Your hands are like the leaves of autumn,
falling softly, enchanting me,
and I lose myself in the way,
in the way that you wander.

Time stops when you walk,
because nothing else really matters,
only watching you,
and feeling that breeze on my face,
breathing the air that you give me.

You, who walks like the wind.
