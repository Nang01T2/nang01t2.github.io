*Por un México Lector* was the personal blog of the Mexican writer Alexia Stuebing Dominguez. The project begins on August 31, 2014 as a space where the expression of art is encouraged through the written word. Here Alexia shares the work of some of her favorite poets like Guillermo Valencia, Laureno Alban, Dámaso Alonso, Vicente Aleixandre, Andrés Bello and Manuel Justo de Rubalcava. 

* [Por Un México Lector](https://porunmexicolector.blogspot.mx/)
