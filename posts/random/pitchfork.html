American music magazine founded by Ryan Schreiber in 1995. The site publishes information and album reviews, focused on the indie genre. PitchFork continues to sell a physical version of their magazine titled The Pitch Fork Review. In the site you can watch interviews, read the news, find new music, discover the latest album reviews, get top music lists or simply read long featured articles or watch photo galleries of many artists.
