# Units

Every physical quantity has an associated dimension, units are the magnitudes of each dimension.

**Units System**

Composed of primary or fundamental units and secondary or derived units. Today most countries use the International System of Units (SI), the United States Customary System (USCS) or the Imperial System of Units.

* In 1875, international standardization treaties made the Metric Unit System, signed by 17 countries in the Metric Convention Treaty with an agreed General Conference of Weights and Measures to be held every 6 years. 
* In the 1960 conference the Metric System was replaced with the SI system with the important addition of the mole in 1971. 
* In the 1987 conference the notation was revised and it was stablished that all units were to be written in lower case with the exception of proper name abbreviations, also the use of a period after such abbreviations was prohibited. 
* The US conversion to the SI system began in 1968 when congress issued an act to study the implications, later a conversion act in 1975 and finally a commercial law in 1988 that had a compulsory limit of 1992, however it the later was never enforced.

**Conversion Factors**

$$
\begin{aligned}
	1\,\text{km/h} = 0.62137\,\text{mile/h} &\rightarrow 1\,\text{mile/h} = 1.6093\,\text{km/h} \\
	1\,\text{cm}^3 = 0.061024\,\text{in}^3 &\rightarrow 1\,\text{in}^3 = 16.387\,\text{cm}^3\\
	1\,\text{W} = 1.341\times 10^{-3}\,\text{hp} &\rightarrow 1\,\text{hp} = 745.7\,\text{W}\\
	1\,\text{W} = 3.413\,\text{Btu/h} &\rightarrow 1\,\text{Btu/h} = 0.293\,\text{W}\\
	1\,\text{m}^3 = 35.315\,\text{ft}^3 &\rightarrow 1\,\text{ft}^3 = 0.028317\,\text{m}^3\\
	1\,\text{N} = 0.22481\,\text{lbf} &\rightarrow 1\,\text{lbf} = 4.4482\,\text{N}\\
	1\,\text{kg} = 2.2046\,\text{lb} &\rightarrow 1\,\text{lb} = 0.4536\,\text{kg}\\
	1\,\text{cm} = 0.3937\,\text{in} &\rightarrow 1\,\text{in} = 2.54\,\text{cm}\\
	1\,\text{m} = 3.2808\,\text{ft} &\rightarrow 1\,\text{ft} = 0.3048\,\text{m}\\
	1\,\text{N} = 0.012\,\text{kgf} &\rightarrow 1\,\text{kgf} = 9.81\,\text{N}\\
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\begin{gathered}
	1\,\text{bar} = 10^{5}\,\text{Pa} = 750.06\,\text{mmHg} = 0.9869\,\text{atm}\\
	1\,\text{atm} = 101325\,\text{Pa} = 14.696\,\text{psi} = 1.01325\,\text{bar}\\\\
	1\,\text{J} = 0.73756\,\text{lbf}\cdot\text{ft} = 9.478 \times 10^{-4}\,\text{Btu}\\
	1\,\text{gal} = 0.13368\,\text{ft}^3 = 3.7854 \times 10^{-3}\,\text{m}^3\\
	1\,\text{lbf/in}^2 = 6894.8\,\text{Pa} = 144\,\text{lbf/ft}^2\\
	1\,\text{Pa} = 1\,\text{N/m}^2 = 1.4504\,\text{lbf/in}^2\\
	1\,\text{L} = 1023\,\text{m}^3 = 0.0353\,\text{ft}^3\\
	1\,\text{kcal} = 4.1868\,\text{kJ}\\
\end{gathered}
$$
